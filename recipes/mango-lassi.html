Mango Lassi

Description

Cool down with the ultimate summer treat: a luscious glass of authentic Mango Lassi. This traditional Indian beverage is a velvety smooth blend of ripe, sweet mangoes, thick yogurt, and a splash of milk. Infused with a hint of warm, aromatic cardamom powder and sweetened to perfection, it offers a delightful balance of creamy, tangy, and fruity flavors. Served chilled and often garnished with slivered nuts or saffron threads, it is the perfect refreshing companion to any Indian meal.

Ingredients

For the Blend:

  • Mango Pulp: 1 cup (preferably sweet varieties like Alphonso or Kesar; fresh or canned)
  • Yogurt (Dahi): 1 cup (plain, whole milk yogurt or Greek yogurt for extra thickness)
  • Milk: ½ cup (chilled, used to adjust the pouring consistency)
  • Sugar or Honey: 2 to 3 tablespoons (adjust based on the sweetness of your mangoes)
  • Cardamom Powder: ¼ teaspoon (freshly ground for the best aroma)
  • Ice Cubes: 4 to 5 cubes (optional, for an extra chilled blend)

For the Garnish (Optional):

  • Pistachios or Almonds: 1 teaspoon, finely slivered
  • Saffron Strands (Kesar): 2 to 3 strands

Steps

Prepare the Ingredients:

  1. Prep Fruit: If using fresh mangoes, peel them, discard the stone, and chop the flesh into small cubes. If using canned pulp, measure out one cup.
  2. Chill: Ensure your yogurt, milk, and mango pulp are thoroughly chilled in the refrigerator before starting for the best refreshing taste.

Blend to Perfection:

  1. Combine: Add the chopped mangoes (or pulp), plain yogurt, milk, sugar, and cardamom powder into a heavy-duty blender jar.
  2. Process: Blend on high speed for 1 to 2 minutes until the mixture turns completely smooth, frothy, and uniform with no fruit lumps remaining.
  3. Adjust: Taste the lassi. If it is too thick, pour in an extra splash of milk and blend for 5 seconds. If it needs more sweetness, add a touch more sugar.

Serve Chilled:

  1. Pour: Drop a couple of fresh ice cubes into your serving glasses if desired, then pour the thick lassi right to the top.
  2. Garnish: Sprinkle the finely slivered pistachios, almonds, and a few strands of saffron over the frothy surface.
  3. Enjoy: Serve immediately while crisp and cold alongside hot, savory dishes.
